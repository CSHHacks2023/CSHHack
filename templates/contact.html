About Earth's Echo

We are a group of students in Rochester, NY, passionate about sustainability, and we're dedicated to providing you with practical tips, informative articles, and resources to help you make a difference. Whether it's reducing waste, conserving energy, supporting local businesses, or adopting eco-friendly practices, we hope to guide you on your sustainability journey.
